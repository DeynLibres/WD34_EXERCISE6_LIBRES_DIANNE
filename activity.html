TOP 10 THINGS TO DO IN BICOL

  1. -See and snorkel near whale sharks in Donsol in Sorsogon.
  2. -Go island-hopping and snorkelling around Caramoan and its islands in the east of Bicol.
  3. -Surf on Catanduanes Island and swim in its waterfalls and lagoons.
  4. -Take an escorted tour by ATV to the active volcano Mount Mayon.
  5. -Explore Bicol’s main city, Legazpi, with its Franciscan church, Spanish pier and Japanese tunnels.
  6. -Tour the Cagsawa Ruins – the atmospheric remnants of a 16th-century Franciscan church.
  7. -Take a boat-ride through the Quitinday Underground River, a natural cave system.
  8. -Visit the imposing Daraga Church, set on a hilltop against a backdrop of Mayon volcano.
  9. -Head for Lignon Hill Nature Park with its zip-line, hiking trail and valley views.
  10. -Relax in Albay Park and Wildlife with its bike hire, lagoon boating and fishing.
